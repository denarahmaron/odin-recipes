Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese. This hearty dish combines the comforting flavors of savory herbs, tangy tomato sauce, and melted cheese, making it a favorite for family dinners and gatherings.

With each layer meticulously built and baked to perfection, lasagna delivers a perfect blend of textures and flavors. The top layer of golden, bubbling cheese adds the finishing touch, making this dish both a visual and culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups (480 ml) canned tomato sauce
  • 1 cup (240 ml) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups (480 ml) béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk mixture)
  • 2 cups (200 g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100 g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup (250 g) ricotta cheese (optional)

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Boil the lasagna sheets in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside. Chop all vegetables and measure out other ingredients.

  2. Cook the Meat Sauce: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the ground beef or sausage and cook until browned. Add onions and garlic, sautéing until softened. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes.

  3. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce: In a separate saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add flour and whisk until smooth. Gradually pour in milk, whisking continuously until thickened. Season with salt and a pinch of nutmeg.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by meat sauce, béchamel, and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna: Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  6. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set. Serve warm with a side of garlic bread or salad. Enjoy!
